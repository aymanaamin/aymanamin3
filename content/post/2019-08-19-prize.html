--- title: ' 2018 International Prize in Statistics is awarded to Bradley Efron' author: Ayman Amin date: '2019-08-19' categories: - Islamic Issues tags: - Statistics - Science - Research summary: International Prize in Statistics slug: Statistics prize ---

Bradley Efron, at Stanford University, has won the US$80,000 “2018 International Prize in Statistics” for his 1970s work on a method for inferring uncertainty in data, by pioneering the ‘bootstrap’ method for measuring the reliability of small data samples, which are now commonly used across many scientific disciplines.
